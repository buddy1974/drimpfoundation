The Blossom Life Academy

At the Blossom Life Academy, we run flagship programs designed for intentional growth — equipping individuals with practical tools and spiritual depth to thrive in purpose.

Our Programs

Flagship Programs

Each program within the Blossom Life Academy is built around a clear outcome: equipping people to go further in their purpose, leadership, and calling.

Program 01

The 7 Pillars of Elevation

A leadership and personal development journey created specifically for women. Our goal is to bring out the best version of you and position you for a more fulfilled expression of your purpose. It unfolds in two sessions before graduation: the first spans 14 weeks, laying a deep foundation; the second is a focused 12-week sprint that sharpens and launches you forward.

Learn More →

Program 02

The School of Marriage Counseling and Coaching (SMCC)

A practical, accelerated training program for those called to support healthy relationships and homes. With three sessions per year, each running just 6 weeks, participants gain real counseling skills with depth and application.

Visit SMCC →

Program 03

The 12 Pillars of Ministry

A comprehensive training track for those called to serve in ministry leadership. Covering foundational principles of pastoral care, spiritual formation, servant leadership, and practical ministry administration, this program equips men and women to stand firm and serve effectively in their divine assignment.
