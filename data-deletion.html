Your Data Rights

Request Data Deletion

How to remove your data from DRIMP Foundation’s systems

Facebook App

Drimp Automation App

The Drimp Automation Facebook app (App ID: 1421484492527175) is used by DRIMP Foundation solely to publish ministry content to our official Facebook Page. This app operates at the Page level only.

No personal Facebook user data is collected, stored, or processed on DRIMP Foundation’s servers through this application. The app does not access user profiles, messages, friend lists, or any other personal user information.

Submit a Request

How to Request Data Removal

If you believe DRIMP Foundation holds any personal data about you and you would like it removed, follow the steps below.

Step 1 Send an email to contact@drimpfoundation.org
Subject Line Data Deletion Request
Include Your full name and any contact details you used when engaging with us (email, phone, or social handle)
Processing Time We will confirm and complete your request within 30 days

Email: contact@drimpfoundation.org
Subject: Data Deletion Request

Facebook Settings

Remove App Permissions on Facebook

You can also revoke the Drimp Automation app’s permissions directly from your own Facebook account at any time. This removes any access the app has to your Facebook account.

Follow these steps on Facebook:

Step 1 Go to Facebook Settings (click your profile picture → Settings & Privacy → Settings)
Step 2 In the left menu, click Apps and Websites
Step 3 Find Drimp Automation in the list of connected apps
Step 4 Click Remove to revoke app permissions

Removing the app from your Facebook settings will immediately revoke any permissions granted. If you need further assistance, contact us at contact@drimpfoundation.org.
