Data & Privacy

Privacy Policy

How DRIMP Foundation collects, uses, and protects your information

Our Commitment

Your Privacy Matters

DRIMP Foundation (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the website drimpfoundation.org. We are committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our site or engages with our ministry. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Organization: Delphine and Rogers Impact Foundation (DRIMP Foundation)
Location: Yaoundé, Cameroon
Contact: contact@drimpfoundation.org

Effective date: April 2026

Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us. This may occur when you:

  • Contact us through our website contact form or by email
  • Register for events, programs, or courses hosted by DRIMP Foundation
  • Engage with our ministry through social media channels

The personal data we may collect includes:

Full Name To identify you and personalise our communications
Email Address To respond to inquiries and send ministry updates
Phone Number For event registration and direct communication where applicable

We do not automatically collect browsing data, cookies, or analytics beyond what is provided by our hosting provider (Vercel).

Data Use

How We Use Your Information

Any personal data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries, questions, or prayer requests
  • To send ministry updates, newsletters, or event announcements (only if you have opted in)
  • To manage event or course registrations and communicate logistics
  • To fulfil obligations arising from any agreement between you and DRIMP Foundation

We will never use your personal information for unrelated commercial purposes.

Social Media

Facebook Page Automation

This website uses a Facebook Page automation tool called Drimp Automation (App ID: 1421484492527175) to publish ministry content to the official Facebook Page “Mah Delphine official”.

This automation tool is used exclusively to schedule and publish posts on our Facebook Page. It operates at the Page level only and does not:

  • Collect, access, or store any personal data from Facebook users
  • Read private messages or profile information of any individual
  • Track interactions, reactions, or comments from users

No personal user data is transmitted to or stored by the Drimp Automation app.

Third Parties

Data Sharing & Disclosure

DRIMP Foundation never sells, rents, or trades your personal information to any third party.

We may share your data with essential service providers solely to operate this website and ministry communications, including:

  • Email providers (e.g., Resend or Gmail) — used to deliver communications you have requested
  • Hosting providers (e.g., Vercel) — who host this website under their own privacy standards

All service providers are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable laws.

We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of DRIMP Foundation, our staff, or others.

Your Rights

Control Over Your Data

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our records
  • Withdraw consent to receive communications from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please send a written request to:

DRIMP Foundation — Data Request
Email: contact@drimpfoundation.org
Subject line: Data Deletion Request or Data Access Request

We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.
