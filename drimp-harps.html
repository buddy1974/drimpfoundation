Drimp Harps

Promoting gospel music as a vehicle for inspiration, worship, and creative expression.

About the Program

Music as Ministry

Drimp Harps is the musical arm of the DRIMP Foundation, created to harness the transformative power of gospel music. We believe music is not merely entertainment — it is ministry, healing, and a bridge between communities. Through Drimp Harps, we cultivate artists, support worship leaders, and bring the sound of hope into every space we occupy.

What We Do

  • Discover and develop gospel music talent across Cameroon and Africa
  • Organize worship events and gospel concerts that inspire communities
  • Support musicians in using their gift as a vehicle for spiritual impact
