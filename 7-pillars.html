The 7 Pillars of Elevation

A leadership and personal development journey for women

About the Program

Bring Out the Best Version of You

This program brings out the best version of each participant and positions her for a more fulfilled expression of purpose. It is structured in two sessions before graduation:

Session 1

14-Week Foundational Phase

Laying a deep foundation in identity, purpose, and leadership. This first session is designed to root each participant in who she is, what she carries, and where she is called to go.

Session 2

12-Week Advanced Sprint

Sharpening skills and launching each participant forward. This focused sprint builds on the foundation laid in Session 1, equipping women with clarity and momentum for their next season.

Both phases are anchored in clarity, community, and application.
