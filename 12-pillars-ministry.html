The 12 Pillars of Ministry

Equipping men and women for effective ministry leadership.

About the Program

Stand Firm. Serve Effectively.

The 12 Pillars of Ministry is a comprehensive training track for those called to serve in ministry leadership. Whether you are just stepping into your assignment or seeking to deepen your foundations, this program gives you the structure, theology, and practical tools to serve effectively and with integrity.

What You Will Cover

1

The Call to Ministry

2

Biblical Foundations of Leadership

3

Pastoral Care and Shepherding

4

Spiritual Formation and Discipline

5

Preaching and Teaching

6

Worship and the Ministry of the Word

7

Church Administration and Governance

8

Counseling and Crisis Support

9

Evangelism and Outreach

10

Family, Marriage, and Community Ministry

11

Servant Leadership and Team Building

12

Vision, Strategy, and Kingdom Impact
