Gatherings & Conferences

Events

Our gatherings are moments of connection, celebration, and transformation. Every event is designed to activate purpose and build community.

The E-Woman Conference

A premier continental gathering celebrating, equipping, and empowering women across generations. An annual event bringing together women leaders, ministers, and professionals for two days of mentorship, ministry, and movement-building.

The Dominion Life Men's Conference

A powerful assembly dedicated to strengthening men in leadership, faith, and purpose. A space where men are challenged to rise, lead with integrity, and build legacies that outlast them.

The Drimp Anniversary Celebration

An annual commemoration of the foundation's journey — honoring the impact made, the lives transformed, and the vision that continues to unfold. A moment to celebrate what God has done and look ahead with faith.

The Feast of the Promise

An annual end-of-year crossover event to pray, celebrate, and commission our communities into a new year with grace and power. A sacred gathering to bless our families and loved ones as they step into all that the next season holds.
