The Zarephath Project

Compassion in action — supporting widows and vulnerable women.

About the Program

Provision, Dignity & Care

Named after the biblical city of Zarephath — a place of provision and encounter — this project extends practical care and emotional support to widows and vulnerable women. We believe no woman should face life's hardest moments alone. The Zarephath Project exists to walk with her, provide for her, and restore her dignity.

What We Do

  • Provide material support and practical assistance to widows in need
  • Create community circles where vulnerable women find belonging and encouragement
  • Connect women to training and economic empowerment opportunities
