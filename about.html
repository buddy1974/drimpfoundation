Who We Are

Our Foundation

The Drimp Foundation (Delphine and Rogers Impact Foundation) is a legally recognized continental institution dedicated to empowerment, leadership development, and community transformation. Rooted in Cameroon, our reach extends across Africa and the diaspora.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To equip individuals, families, and communities with the tools, training, and support they need to rise into their purpose — spiritually, professionally, and socially.

Our Vision

A continent of empowered leaders, restored families, and transformed communities — where every person has access to structured pathways of growth.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Empowerment

We believe every person carries untapped potential. Our programs are designed to unlock it.

Integrity

All we do is grounded in accountability, transparency, and moral excellence.

Community

We build together. No program exists in isolation — everything connects back to family and community.

Faith

Our work is rooted in faith — a conviction that transformation is possible for every life we touch.

Our Story

How It Began

Founded by Apostle Rogers Nforgwei and Rev. Delphine Nforgwei, the DRIMP Foundation was born from a vision to create structured pathways of impact across Africa. From gospel music to leadership academies, from marriage counseling to women's conferences — every initiative reflects one heartbeat: people matter, and transformation is possible.
