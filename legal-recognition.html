Legally Recognized

Delphine and Rogers Impact Foundation (DRIMP Foundation) is a legally recognized association registered under Law No. 90/053 of 19 December 1990 on Freedom of Association in Cameroon.

Full Name Delphine and Rogers Impact Foundation (DRIMP Foundation)
Legal Framework Law No. 90/053 of 19 December 1990
Registration No. 00253
Date of Recognition 19 February 2026
Headquarters Yaoundé, Cameroon
Country Republic of Cameroon

What This Means

Institutional Accountability

As a legally recognized association, the DRIMP Foundation operates with full institutional accountability under Cameroonian law. Our legal status enables us to formalize partnerships, receive funding, and operate programs at a continental scale with credibility and transparency.
